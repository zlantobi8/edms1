Register of Capabilities

A complete toolkit for every role in the exam hall

From setting up the academic structure to reviewing footage after the exam — everything runs on one local server.

Administrator — EMDMS/ADM

Full control over exams, users, and results

ADM—01

Academic structure

Faculties, departments, sessions, semesters, classes and subjects — all managed in one place.

ADM—02

Student & invigilator management

Register students with passport photos, auto-generate credentials, lock/unlock accounts, and reset passwords.

ADM—03

Exam & question builder

Create exams with scheduling, pass marks, and randomization. Build questions manually or import via CSV.

ADM—04

Results & reporting

Filter results by exam, department or class. Export to CSV or a printable PDF result sheet.

ADM—05

Recording & incident review

Browse, play, download, or delete any student's surveillance recording. Review a per-exam incident report with severity flags.

ADM—06

Backup & restore

One-click database backups you can download, plus a guided restore flow if you ever need to roll back.

Invigilator — EMDMS/INV

See everything, miss nothing

INV—01

Live monitoring grid

Every student writing an exam shown at once, with name, reg. number, progress, and online status.

INV—02

Live webcam feeds

Request a student's live camera feed on demand over a direct peer-to-peer connection — no cloud video service involved.

INV—03

Warnings & controls

Send a warning message, lock a student's account, or force-submit an exam directly from the dashboard.

INV—04

AI-assisted flags

No-face, multiple-faces, head-turned-away, and unusual-noise signals surface live during monitoring.

INV—05

Search & filter

Quickly find a specific student by name or registration number in a large exam hall.

INV—06

Post-exam review

Review recordings and the incident report for any assigned exam afterward to catch anything missed live.

Student — EMDMS/STU

A focused, guided exam experience

STU—01

Live countdown timer

Always-visible timer that turns red in the final minute, with automatic submission when time runs out.

STU—02

Question navigator

Jump to any question instantly, mark questions for review, and see answered/unanswered status at a glance.

STU—03

Autosave

Every answer is saved to the server the moment it's selected — no "save" button, no lost progress.

STU—04

Reconnection handling

If the connection drops mid-exam, the app detects it, reconnects automatically, and resumes exactly where you left off.

STU—05

Full-screen lock

The exam runs in full-screen with copy/paste, right-click, and multi-tab access disabled throughout.

STU—06

Duplicate-submission protection

Once submitted, an exam cannot be re-entered or resubmitted — guaranteed at the server level.
